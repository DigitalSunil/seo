# AI SEO Guide

## Introduction

AI SEO is the practice of optimizing websites for both traditional search engines and AI-powered search platforms.

Modern search users discover information through AI systems such as Google AI Overview, ChatGPT, Gemini, Microsoft Copilot, and Perplexity.

This guide explains how to improve visibility across these platforms.

---

## What is AI SEO?

AI SEO combines traditional SEO with Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO).

The objective is to create content that is:

- Helpful
- Accurate
- Well structured
- Easy for AI systems to understand

---

## AI SEO Best Practices

- Create original content
- Build topical authority
- Publish detailed guides
- Use FAQ sections
- Add structured data
- Improve EEAT
- Keep content updated
- Build a strong brand entity
