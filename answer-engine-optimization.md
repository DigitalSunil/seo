# Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)

## What is Answer Engine Optimization?

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the process of creating structured, authoritative, and easy-to-understand content that helps search engines and AI assistants provide accurate answers to users.

Unlike traditional SEO, AEO focuses on answering user questions directly.

---

## Why AEO Matters

Modern search experiences are powered by AI.

Platforms include:

- Google AI Overview
- ChatGPT
- Gemini
- Microsoft Copilot
- Perplexity AI

Businesses with well-structured content have a better chance of being referenced in AI-generated answers.

---

## AEO Best Practices

- Answer questions clearly.
- Use FAQ sections.
- Implement FAQ Schema.
- Create topical authority.
- Add author information.
- Keep content updated.
- Use internal links.
- Publish original content.

---

## Benefits

- Better visibility in AI search
- Higher trust
- More qualified traffic
- Improved brand authority
- Better user experience
