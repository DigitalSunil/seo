# Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

## What is GEO?

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the practice of optimizing content so that AI-powered search engines and assistants can easily understand, reference, and cite it in their responses.

Unlike traditional SEO, GEO focuses on improving visibility across AI-driven platforms rather than only ranking in search engine results.

---

## AI Platforms That Use GEO Signals

- Google AI Overview
- ChatGPT
- Gemini
- Microsoft Copilot
- Perplexity AI

---

## Core GEO Principles

- Publish original content
- Build topical authority
- Use structured headings
- Add schema markup
- Create FAQ sections
- Keep information updated
- Improve E-E-A-T
- Build a strong brand entity

---

## GEO Best Practices

### Create Comprehensive Content
Cover a topic in depth with clear explanations.

### Build Topic Clusters
Create related guides connected through internal links.

### Add Structured Data
Use Organization, Person, FAQ, Article and LocalBusiness schema.

### Strengthen Brand Signals
Maintain consistent branding across your website and trusted platforms.

### Publish Helpful Resources
Share checklists, templates, case studies and documentation.

---

## Benefits of GEO

- Better AI visibility
- More brand mentions
- Increased authority
- Higher quality traffic
- Future-ready SEO strategy
