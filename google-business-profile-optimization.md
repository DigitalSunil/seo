# Google Business Profile Optimization

## Overview

Google Business Profile (GBP) Optimization helps local businesses improve their visibility in Google Search and Google Maps.

A well-optimized profile increases calls, website visits, direction requests, and customer trust.

## Benefits

- Higher Google Maps Rankings
- Better Local Visibility
- More Phone Calls
- More Website Visits
- Increased Customer Reviews
- Better Local Brand Authority

## Optimization Checklist

- Complete Business Information
- Primary & Secondary Categories
- Business Description
- Products & Services
- High-Quality Images
- Weekly Google Posts
- Customer Reviews
- Questions & Answers
- Appointment Links
- Local Keywords
- NAP Consistency

## Best Practices

- Keep business information updated.
- Respond to every customer review.
- Publish regular updates and offers.
- Upload fresh business photos.
- Monitor profile insights regularly.
